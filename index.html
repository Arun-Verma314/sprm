📝 Mission & Origin Blueprint

Welcome to the official repository of the Seminar on Pure and Rigorous Mathematics (SPRM). This framework is a student-led initiative designed from the ground up by senior MSc Mathematics scholars at IIT Kanpur to solve an immediate, repeating cultural bottleneck: surviving the sheer transition shock of your first semester.
