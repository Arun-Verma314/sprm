📖 Mission Statement & Origin History

Welcome to the official digital hub of the Seminar on Pure and Rigorous Mathematics (SPRM). This workspace is a student-led repository engineered from scratch by senior MSc Mathematics scholars at IIT Kanpur to solve an immediate, structural hurdle: mastering postgraduate mathematical abstraction without losing confidence.

Why I Initiated SPRM

As an MSc Mathematics candidate currently navigating the intense workload of our department, I saw that arriving juniors are frequently blind-sided by the jump from undergraduate calculation to abstract analysis. Textbooks print final, clean steps while hiding the discovery process. This seminar is explicitly dedicated to exposing that hidden scratch work.

Faculty Mentorship

Formulated and run under the active guidance and logistical support of Prof. P. Muthukumar, SPRM acts as an academic bridge. Prof. Muthukumar’s dedication to teaching clarity has allowed us to turn these peer review logs into an active, helpful departmental resource.
